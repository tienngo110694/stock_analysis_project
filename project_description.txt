overall flow: 
0: get financial data of stock on HOSE, stock market in Vietnam and insert into ClickHouse database
1: chat in Vietnamese natural language about the stock code and request, on discord.
2.1: it triggers flow on n8n, which will get financial data from ClickHouse database and feed into gemini nodes, which will analyze financial data.
2.2: another Gemini node in charge of searching for latest news about macro economy and industry of the stock in Vietnam.
2.3: another Gemini node in charge of searching for latest news about the stock code.
3: output of 2.1, 2.2, 2.3, will be feed into another gemini node, which is the node summarize all data 
  , analyze and give advice. It should contains buy/sell/neutral at specific price 
  , reasons which's structured into 3 parts: financial analysis, macro economy analysis, industry analysis.
  , and finally output the result into discord. 
4. Output from step 3 will be feed into Ollama to embed into vector database (ClickHouse),
    and use it to answer questions about stock analysis in the future.
